Medicinal Waters

by Dina Moreno

Soda Springs is a historic place. Many years ago, emigrants traveling the Oregon Trail reached this place and used the waters for medicinal purposes.

Alkaline water has some health benefits, but you need to be careful not to drink too much; some of the benefits are: increase energy, slows aging, improves metabolism, and digestion.

Soda Springs is a landmark in history. But not all is history; nowadays, people come to visit the Soda Springs Geyser, this natural phenomenon discovered in 1937 attracts people from all over the country. Also, people here enjoy the Geyser Park. The Park has different amenities, such as picnic tables, and a hotel. Relaxing around the medicinal waters of Soda Springs is a must if you're traveling nearby the area.
