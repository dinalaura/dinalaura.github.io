Western Rodeo

by Dina Moreno

The Preston Rodeo has 84 years of tradition, and tickets are already on sale. The organizers are looking for volunteers. Everything is getting ready to continue with the traditional rodeo. There are activities exclusively for children's participation, a great way to grow traditions in new generations.

But to be honest, I don't know a lot of rodeos, I grew up in northern Mexico, yes, we do have rodeos here, but my country is famous for "Charrerías." Tricky word, right? Those are similar to rodeos, I guess. A good practitioner of that Mexican sport loves his horse more than anything, has a large hat, sings well, and is extremely attractive, all those attributes according to Mexican movies!

What do you think? Rodeos and "Charrería" are similar? Maybe not, but humanity is always looking for a connection with nature and animals. I think that's something every culture has, and we need to take care of it.
