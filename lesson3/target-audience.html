Description of the target audience

The audience of the howsoutside.com web site is described here:
Who: Digital Nomads, Developers, Entrepreneurs, and others
interested in being informed about the weather.
Age: 21 to 40.
Technology: The users prefer tablets and mobile devices,
rather than desktop computers.
Income: they salary is about $60,000 annually.
Motivation: They want to travel and ensure their journeys will be safe.

Personas

Uta Schulz

  • Occupation: Web Developer.
  • Demographics and Education: 40 years old. Has a Master's level education in Technology.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: She is traveling due to work and requires to confirm visiting in a city with good weather.
  • Social: She likes to exercise and being outdoors in the forest in Bavaria where she lives.
  • Technology: She uses mostly her iPhone and her MacBook Pro. Also, she uses Google Docs online and Adobe software.

Mahmut Gorecki

  • Occupation: Software Engineer.
  • Demographics and Education: 35 years old. Has a Bachelor's level education in Computer Science.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: He is a digital nomad and wants to move abroad during the summer to a sunny place.
  • Social: He enjoys nature. He goes for a walk frequently in Istanbul where he lives.
  • Technology: He mostly uses his iPad Pro. For the phone device, he prefers the Samsung Flip. He uses his iPad camera extensively. Also, he likes to draw over his photographs.

Scenarios

  1. Mahmut wants to take a walk in nature, but he is not sure if the weather is good to get his gadgets with him.
  2. Uta is planning an outdoor event to code with her team, she needs to plan it on a sunny day.
  3. Uta is traveling to Berlin for work, she wants to know which clothes to pack.
  4. Uta wants to build a rain photo stock for a new website, she needs to know the rain probability.
  5. Mahmut is moving to another country for the winter, he is a digital nomad. He wants to ski so he needs to know the better place for that.
