PARC: Contrast

Peter Mckinnon | Photographer

Peter Mckinnon, a well-known YouTuber, uses contrast in his website to make a statement with photography. The image and the text work with each other thanks to the use of this design element.

PARC: Alignment

Nintendo

Nintendo's website has a menu in its Spanish version of the site, aligned in a grid, it's easily recognized. It looks well organized and clean, and every item follows the same line.

PARC: Repetition

Duolingo

Duolingo website uses repetition in various elements, such as in language skills. Every level is an icon surrounded by a circle. The design changes according to the topic, but the figure follows a pattern, which is easy to recognize.
